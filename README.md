# Uber Ride Analytics

Analysis of 150,000 Uber rides using Python, Pandas, and Seaborn.

## Dataset
- 150,000 rides
- 21 columns covering bookings, fares, ratings, cancellations, and payment

## Questions Answered
1. What hour of day has the most rides and highest average fare?
2. Which vehicle type generates the most revenue?
3. What is the most common reason customers cancel rides?
4. Do higher rated drivers get better customer ratings back?
5. Which payment method is most popular and does it vary by vehicle type?

## Key Insights
- 6pm is peak hour but fare stays flat — no effective surge pricing
- Auto dominates revenue through volume (23k rides) not price
- 44% of customer cancellations are actually driver faults
- Driver and customer ratings have zero correlation (-0.001)
- UPI dominates across all vehicle types platform wide

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
